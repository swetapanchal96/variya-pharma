Rapid Mixer Granulator

Rapid Mixer Granulator (RMG) is one of the most essential machines used in pharmaceutical solid dosage manufacturing. It is designed to achieve uniform mixing and wet granulation of powders in a single process, ensuring consistent product quality, reduced processing time and enhanced production efficiency.

Engineered for GMP-compliant operations, the machine delivers high-speed mixing with superior granule formation while minimizing material loss and operator intervention.

The main impeller has dual-speed 750/1500 RPM synchronous motor connected through a belt drive with worm reduction gearbox and chain coupling.
Main impeller provides efficient mixing of material at the center and bottom of the bowl.
Bowl and beaters are manufactured in SS 304 and designed in three sections consisting of flat bottom, cylindrical middle portion and conical top.
Chopper blade is equipped with a dual-speed 1500/3000 RPM synchronous motor and features four stainless steel blades.
Mounting platform is constructed from robust M.S. channel frame with stainless steel staircase, railings and fully covered side panels.
Control panel includes discharge opening and closing controls, auto/manual operation, ON/OFF controls, ammeter, voltmeter, air regulator, pressure gauge, air filter, timer and indicator lights.
Pneumatic system is provided for smooth operation of the discharge valve during opening and closing cycles.

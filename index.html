Rapid Mixer Granulator (RMG)

  • Dual speed 1500/3000 RPM 4-blade chopper.
  • 3-section bowl: Flat bottom, cylindrical middle, conical top.
  • Integrated robust M.S. frame with fully closed SS cladding.
Standard System Volume Range: 25 Liters to 240 Liters

Fluid Bed Dryer (FBD)

  • Entirely built out of premium SS 304 or SS 316 alloy.
  • Expansion chamber features integrated filter finger bags.
  • Pneumatic cylinder container lifting prevents floor line leakage.
Dryer Load Range Capacity: 30-50 Kg up to 200-220 Kg
View Technical Details

Octagonal Blender & Mass Mixer

  • Slow-speed processing configuration delivers uniform dry mixes.
  • Safety dust cover made out of transparent poly-carbonate.
  • Self-adjusting seals completely eliminate carbon drop risk.
Max Blending Volume: 375 Liters to 2200 Liters Gross

Multi Mill, Colloid Mill & Shifters
